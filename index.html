Hello, my name is Collin Wong. I am a goal oriented individual who is always looking for ways to expand my skills and knowledge. My goal is to become a Full Stack Web Developer who creates projects with great UI/UX design as well as a quick and efficient code base. As the Lead Back-End Developer for TheCubeTube, which is a YouTube content management application, I was responsible for building the server and database with PHP and MySQL. Additionally, I was also the Lead Front-End Developer for the hackathon winning Pokemon-Speed-Battle, which is a competitive multiplayer game based on two of my childhood favorite games, Pokemon and Dance Dance Revolution. As a leader and teammate, I believe that strong communication and having a shared vision leads to the best outcomes.


On my free time you’ll find me at the gym, playing video games or on youtube, watching videos about random science and history facts.
